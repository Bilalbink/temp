ABOUT OUTLETTER

Outletter is a cross-platform mobile application that will act as the perfect shopping companion and will help the user make informed choices about the article they’re trying to buy. The app will utilise AR and a cloud-based, real-time object recognition engine to identify the objects the device is being pointed at and render metadata about the product like the best price and other pricing options at different stores

FEATURES

Get the best possible deals!

Outletter finds the best possible prices of the product at different stores and will help the user make informed choices about the product they’re trying to buy

See offers in your location!

Outletter will allow the users to be able to see geotagged deals and offers on products at the store they’re currently in
